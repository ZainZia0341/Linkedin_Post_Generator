Most teams do not need more tools.

They need clearer habits around the tools they already have.

Here is the pattern I keep seeing:
1. The problem is fuzzy.
2. The workflow gets automated too early.
3. Nobody reviews the output.

Start with the problem.
Make the workflow boring.
Then automate the part that repeats.

That is how AI becomes useful instead of noisy.
